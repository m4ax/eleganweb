## Understanding Data Ingestion

Before diving into the intricacies of innovative methods for data ingestion, it's crucial to understand what data ingestion really entails.

**Data ingestion** is the process of importing, transferring, loading, and processing data for immediate use or storage in a database. This process allows businesses to analyze data, providing actionable insights and supporting data-driven decisions.
